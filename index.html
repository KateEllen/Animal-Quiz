How To Play

  • What Dog Is This: A game where a picture is given and you choose what type of dog is in the picture
  • Animal Trivia: A true or false quiz, test your animal knowledge!
  • Aligator VS Crocodile: A game where a picture is shown and you decide if it's an aligator or a crocodile.

Each game has only 5 questions, but there's a wide variety of questions so if you play again, you may not get repeated questions.
